Reason for Major:

Computer science wasn't always my first choice as a major. If anything, it wasn't one I considered until I was signing up to colleges last year, 2024. Not because it never interested me, but mainly because I never thought about it. I did love computers tho. I loved coding little things every now and then, but I believed that what I wanted to do with my life was acting, or art. But soon that wish became very unrealistic, so I gave it up and I ended up confused. I didn't know what I wanted to do anymore. Until one night speaking with my father about my confusion, he told me I should consider it, because it's something that will be needed always. We will always need programmers. And I thought about it. And he was right, I do love coding, although I didn't do it much I had coded simple beginner games and trained myself on C#. So after realizing that maybe this is the right major for me, I joined a coding club, learned HTML, got a MacBook Pro, best model I could find so it would stay future proof. It was expensive but it was worth it because I love my major, I enjoy doing it, and I feel like I made the best decision by choosing it.
